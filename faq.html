FAQ

Q: What do I do if my item is damaged or not as described?
A: If your item arrives damaged or isn’t as described, you can contact the seller directly using their contact information on their page to resolve the issue.


Q: Can I cancel or change my order after purchasing?
A: You can contact the seller to request a cancellation or change, but it depends on the seller's policies. Some sellers allow order changes or cancellations within a certain timeframe, while others may not.


Q: Do I need a Boiler Business account to purchase on Boiler Business?
A: While you don’t need a Boiler Business account to purchase, having an account allows you to track orders, save favorite items or businesses, and receive updates from shops you follow.


Q: What are Boiler Business's fees?
A: Boiler Business charges a $0.20 listing fee per item, plus a 5% transaction fee on the sale price, and a 3% + $0.25 payment processing fee. Additional fees may apply for certain services like ads and shipping labels.


Q: Can I cancel or change my order after purchasing?
A: You can contact the seller to request a cancellation or change, but it depends on the seller's policies. Some sellers allow order changes or cancellations within a certain timeframe, while others may not.


Q: How do I set up taxes for my shop?
A: Boiler Business automatically calculates and collects sales tax in certain regions, but you are responsible for setting up tax settings for other locations where you have tax obligations. You can manage this in your shop's settings.


Q: What if my order doesn't arrive or is delayed?
A: If your order hasn’t arrived within the expected timeframe, first check the tracking information to see if it’s still in transit. If it’s delayed or missing, contact the seller directly.


Q: What if I accidentally purchased the wrong item or quantity?
A: If you've made a mistake in your order, contact the seller as soon as possible. If the item hasn't been shipped yet, many sellers will accommodate order changes or cancellations.


Q: What should I do if I don't like the item I purchased?
A: Boiler Business allows buyers to leave reviews and communicate directly with sellers. If you don’t like the item or it isn’t what you expected, you can message the seller to discuss a return or exchange, depending on the seller’s shop policy.


Q: Can I offer discounts to repeat buyers?
A: Yes, Boiler Business allows you to set up discounts for your buyers. You can create coupon codes that provide percentage discounts, free shipping, or other offers.
